Our story

Techblysis, established in 2017, is a dynamic company with a team of over 50+ highly skilled engineers, specializing in various cutting-edge technologies. With a track record of serving more than 50 successful clients, we have earned a reputation for delivering exceptional solutions tailored to their unique requirements.

Our expertise encompasses a wide range of services, including Cloud solutions, Data Analytics, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Data Science. Additionally, we excel in Embedded Software and IoT development, crafting innovative solutions for the connected world.

When it comes to user experience, our UI/UX specialists ensure that every product we create delights users and maximizes engagement. Moreover, our capabilities span across a full spectrum of software development, allowing us to cater to diverse industry needs with precision and proficiency.

Headquartered in Chennai, Techblysis continues to expand its capabilities and remain at the forefront of the tech industry, helping organizations worldwide achieve their goals through advanced technology and expert solutions.

Company values

Empathy
At Techblysis, we believe empathy is crucial for our company's success, our clients' satisfaction, and the well-being of every individual within our team. We genuinely care about the impact of our work, our clients' needs, and the collaboration among our teammates. Our culture fosters curiosity, encouraging us to look at situations from different perspectives, leading to high commitment and strong collaboration.
Openness
Innovation and growth thrive in an environment of open communication, honesty, and trust. At Techblysis, we eliminate bureaucracy, hidden agendas, and favoritism.
